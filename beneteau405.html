Beneteau 405



This boat is the charter version of the well known Beneteau 400.
The Beneteau 405 is very similar to our Beneteau 393 with the same layout, but the 405 being slightly larger has more storage and carries more water and fuel. 
There are three double cabins and two heads/showers, with one being en suite to the forward cabin.
"Boheme" will sleep eight at a pinch, as the saloon table converts to a double berth.
