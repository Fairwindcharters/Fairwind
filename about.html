Kim and Greta have a wide variety of sailing experience, both on the New Zealand coast and abroad. Between us we have over 100,000 sea miles under our belts. The two of us have circumnavigated the globe and Kim’s experience goes back as far as being second mate under Captain Bligh on the Bounty! We both hold New Zealand commercial skippers licences.


Fairwind was started after global charter company The Moorings, for whom Kim had managed the Bay of Islands base for some years, withdrew from New Zealand in 2003. We perceived an opening for a smaller bareboat charter company that still offered vessels of a high standard. Kim’s involvement in the charter industry dates back to the early 80’s. 
Both of us have sailed the world yet we agree that the Northland Coast, with its beauty of scenery and variety of sailing, easily matches anywhere we have visited. We appreciate being able to call the Bay of Islands our home. We hope you will accept our invitation to charter with FairWind, and share this alluring cruising ground with us.

P.S. That Bounty voyage was in 1989, not 1789, and was a re-enactment of the original ‘mutiny’ voyage, with the four-times-great grandson of the first Bligh in command.
