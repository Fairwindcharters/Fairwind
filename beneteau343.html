Beneteau 343

From the design house of Berret/Recoupeau comes this popular 35 footer. Relatively high freeboard and minimal wetted surface gives a large volume interior combined with good sailing characteristics. Plenty of light from the overhead ports ensures an airy open saloon, while the wide cockpit and innovative pivoting wheel gives excellent traffic flow to the transom.
The accommodation comprises of a wide athwartships berth in the after cabin and a smaller vee berth in the cabin forward. A large shower stall and heads is set to starboard.
Suitable for two couples for up to a week.
"Alomar" was launched in 2005 and presents in very good condition.
