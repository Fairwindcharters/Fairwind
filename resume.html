Fairwind Charters Skipper Resume and Crew List.

Please complete this form before making any charter payment. Feel free to discuss with us any aspects you are unsure of.

COMPETENCY: For the safety of our clients and our yachts, it is important that our bareboat charterers be well qualified. We determine this by carefully reviewing resumes of the designated skipper and the most experienced crew member. We are looking for well-rounded sailing experience and familiarity with boats of the approximate size and type you intend to charter. Completion of recognised boat courses is helpful but not mandatory. Practical knowledge and experience in navigation and anchoring procedures is essential. If we need more information than shown on your resume, we will give you a call to talk it over. We will accept your reservation based upon the information you provide. If on your arrival at our charter base we determine that you lack the experience to safely handle the boat of your choice we reserve the right to place a skipper of our choosing on board at your expense.
