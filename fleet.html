Fairwind Charters Fleet

Below is our fleet of yachts for hire in the Bay of Islands.
Follow the thumbnail links for detailed information on each boat.

Beneteau 285

The baby of our fleet is this well known 29 footer from the Beneteau stable. With a double cabin aft and another smaller double forward it is a very roomy boat for its size. Best suited for a couple or a family with two smaller children. .....

Beneteau 320

The bigger sister to the Beneteau 285, with the increased volume giving more space in the cabins and saloon. This is our smallest boat comfortable for two couples for shorter charters. 

Beneteau 343

Also known as the Moorings 352, this 35 footer is another well known boat from the Beneteau stable and a popular bareboat charter boat throughout the world. The boat has a wide double cabin aft and smaller forward so is suitable for two couples for up to a week.

Beneteau 393

Our smallest three cabin boat is similar to the larger Beneteau 40 but has slighty smaller cabins and much less storage. The 393 offers room for three couples for shorter charters. Three double cabin layout with two heads and showers, one en suite to the forward cabin.

Beneteau 40 

This 40 footer is also known as the Moorings 413, is our newest three double cabin yacht in our fleet. There are two double cabins aft and a larger double forward, plus two heads and showers, with one en suite to the forward cabin. 

Elan 434

No other bareboat this well equipped in the Bay of Islands! Genset, bow thruster, in boom furling main and even a diesel heater to take the chill off those winter nights!  Two double cabins and a twin berth cabin and plenty of room, making this boat suitable for up to six people for longer charters. Two heads/showers.
