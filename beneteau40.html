Beneteau 40


This 40 footer offers room for three couples for short charters or a larger family or two couples for longer charters . This version of the Beneteau 40 is also known as the Moorings 413 and comes from the respected French design team of Berret/Racoupeau.
Two showers and heads, with one en suite to the forward cabin.
"Shard" was launched in 2008.
