Here's a few reasons why.

Our fleet is very well maintained.
Because we have a long term relationship with our owners (some who have been with us since 2003), our maintenance and upkeep is on-going.

We've got the best choice of boats.
At Fairwind we have the widest range of bareboat yachts available for charter in the Bay of Islands. If you can find another yacht elsewhere of the same specifications it will be from an agent. Booking direct with us ensures that you dealing with someone who knows exactly what they are talking about.

We're not afraid to compare ourselves with the opposition!
Fairwind is one of just two bareboat fleet operators in the Bay of Islands. The others is Great Escape, with yachts from 20-31 feet.

We know what we're about.
Fairwind is a loaclly owned and operated company and we have an intimate knowledge of our product, both of the boats and of the area. We won't try to sell you something that you don't need, or try to convince you that our boats are suitable for something they are not.

Our goal is to give you a trouble free holiday and we consider that our product offers the best value for money!
