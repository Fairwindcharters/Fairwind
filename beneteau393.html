Beneteau 393



Also known as the Moorings 403, this roomy 39 footer is our smallest three cabin boat.
The Beneteau 393 lines are penned by the respected French design team of Berret/Racoupeau and give a large volume vessel that still performs well and is easily capable of longer passage making.
There are three double cabins and two heads/showers, with one being en suite to the forward cabin.
"The Good Life" will sleep three couples comfortably on shorter charters, yet is easily handled by two people for longer passages.
