Fairwind New Zealand Yacht Charters offers well presented yachts for bareboat charter in the Bay of Islands

The fleet in our family company includes yachts for charter from 28-feet to 44-feet. This is the widest selection of bareboat yachts available in the Bay of Islands. Our boats can be recognized by the high standard of their maintenance and presentation. Better maintenance is our calling card and our goal is to give you a trouble free sailing holiday.

Our Fleet

Our bareboat charter yacht fleet is based in the port of Opua at the heart of the Bay of Islands, New Zealand's premier cruising destination. 
Although bareboat (sail yourself) charters are our primary focus, our yachts are also available with a skipper or cook.

Cruising Area

The area in which our boats are licensed for bareboat charter extends from Cape Karikari, 60 miles north of the Bay of Islands to Cape Colville in the south. This is 170 miles of the best sailing that New Zealand has to offer.
