Beneteau 320

The Philippe Briand designed Oceanis 320 is the bigger sister to the First 285. A similar layout below, but the increased volume gives more room in the cabins and saloon. A pull out berth in the saloon allows the boat to sleep six at a pinch, although it is better suited to one or two couples. Our 320 comes with spray dodger and bimini.
Like the First 285, our 320 was launched in 1991, making her an old boat by world standards but she is well maintained by us and we know that you will not find a newer (or better presented) boat of this size for hire in the Bay of Islands.
