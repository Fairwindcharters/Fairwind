We don't share information on our clients with anyone.

The only database we maintain on our clients is for the purposes of our booking schedule and any personal information gathered is for this purpose or for our statutory recording obligations under the New Zealand Maritime Rules.

We won't be bothering you with newsletters or promotions.

The only publicly displayed information on our clients is on our testimonials page and this information is freely given for public display by those clients.
